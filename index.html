About

Ramathibodi Neonatal Library is an educational platform developed by the Division of Neonatology, Department of Pediatrics, Faculty of Medicine Ramathibodi Hospital, Mahidol University.

The library is designed to support Neonatology Fellows, Pediatric Residents, Medical Students, and healthcare professionals by providing centralized access to neonatal clinical guidelines, landmark publications, educational resources, and teaching videos.

Our goal is to promote evidence-based practice, enhance neonatal education, and facilitate lifelong learning through reliable and up-to-date resources.

How to use

The library is organized into four main sections:

📘 Guidelines

Browse Ramathibodi neonatal clinical guidelines organized by specialty. These evidence-based PDF documents serve as practical references for daily clinical practice.

📚 Important Publications

Explore landmark neonatal publications categorized by organ system to support evidence-based learning and journal review.

🎓 Educational Topics & References

Access curated educational materials, including teaching slides, board review resources, and recommended references for neonatal medicine.

🎥 Videos

Watch procedure demonstrations, teaching sessions, clinical skills, and educational videos designed to complement neonatal training.

Use the search function or browse by category to quickly find the information you need.
